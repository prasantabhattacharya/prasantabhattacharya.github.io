--- title: "Online Search: Modeling information seeking on digital platforms" excerpt: "Re-imagining online search: Characterizing web search tasks using large scale search logs, designing methods to model complex tasks (e.g. plan wedding)
" collection: portfolio --- Through this set of projects we try to analyze large scale data on anonymized search logs understand how users search for information online to accomplish specific tasks. Re-visiting online search through the lens of search tasks lets search engines perform better personalization, ad targeting and task-aware ranking of search results.
